Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses do you work with?
We primarily serve established businesses with $5M–$100M in annual revenue across a wide range of industries including manufacturing, logistics, healthcare, technology, and professional services.
How fast can I receive funding?
Depending on the type of capital and your documentation readiness, funding can be deployed in as little as 48 hours for bridge and asset-based solutions. SBA loans typically take 2–4 weeks.
Will applying affect my credit score?
No. Our initial qualification process uses a soft inquiry only. A hard credit pull is only performed if you choose to move forward with a specific funding offer.
What if my credit score is low?
We specialize in working with businesses that have challenged credit profiles. Our family-office network and alternative lending partners offer solutions that traditional banks typically won't consider.
What are your fees?
Our success fees range from 3–10% depending on the deal size, complexity, and type of capital. All fees are disclosed upfront before you commit to any funding arrangement.
